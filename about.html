Who are we


We are a group of Pro-Players from South America, that used to play on Contenders and gave our best in every match. But on February the 5th of 2021, Blizzard announced that contenders would no longer be available in South America and the Pacific region. Now we are some of the biggest boosters in the market, with 2 years of experience. We are always trying to get a better and faster service for our costumers.

Our Dream


Our dream is to keep playing the game. We love it since its launch in 2016, and at the same time hopefully make money so it becomes our job. Instead of having to work in companies and stores, we want to keep doing what we love and make it profitable.
